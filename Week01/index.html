Easy Spaghetti Recipe

This is a simple, fast recipe for spaghetti with meat sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 box of Barilla Pronto Half-Cut Spaghetti, for garnishing, and freshly
  • ground beef
  • a jar of your favorite tomato sauce
  • fresh basil
  • grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions
  2. Brown the ground beef over medium-high heat, breaking it up with a spatula as it cooks
  3. After beef has cooked through, add the pasta sauce and stir to combine. Continue cooking until the meat sauce is completely heated through.
  4. After the pasta has finished cooking, plate the pasta and add sauce
  5. Garnish each plate with basil
